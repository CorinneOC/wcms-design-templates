iPhone & Android Apps

This mobile applications provide a connection to the University of California, Santa Cruz — wherever you are.

Get the iPhone or Android App
It's free.

Android MarketplaceApp Store

This is only Version 2.0 of UCSC's mobile apps. We anticipate adding additional features. So stay tuned.

This application was designed and developed by iCampus, in collaboration with UCSC's University Relations division. UCSC's Information Technology Services, Business & Administrative Services, and Student Affairs divisions also provided key consulting support.

Support
For support, send email to: mobileapps@ucsc.edu

Don't Have an iPhone or Android?
Click here to access an online version of our mobile application with any smart phone.

Use this UCSC mobile application to access:
  • News
    Get the latest information about UCSC's people and programs.
  • Maps
    See a map of the campus, and your GPS proximity in relationship to your desired location.
  • Videos
    Check out campus videos from UCSC's YouTube site.
  • Photos
    Take a look at UCSC's people, architecture, and stunning setting.
  • Events
    UCSC activities are listed on the campus's online calendar.
  • Emergency
    A special feed can provide information during times of emergency.
  • Library
    Get information about the library, such as location, contact info, and hours.
  • Directory
    Search the campus directory for fellow classmates, faculty, or staff. Contact them directly from the app.
