About UCSC

About: This is a place like no other -- a place that inspires, a place that evokes passion and imagination -- all with a view to a limitless horizon.

Facts & Figures

UCSC opened in 1965 with 650 students. Forty-eight years later, the campus is home to 15,400 undergraduate and 1,400 graduate students.

Administration
